About the Beta
PhysIndex is currently under continuous development, but anyone is free to access the project in its current state today. As you use PhysIndex, please note that what you see is neither complete nor guaranteed to be accurate. You can help us in this development phase by contacting us about any bugs or inaccuracies that you discover.
Current Status
As of February 2, 2014 the variables, constants, equations, and units available in the PhysIndex database are consistent with most of the material covered in an introductory physics class. This material is primarily classical mechanics. For the most part, the descriptions of the variables and equations have not been rigorously fact-checked.
