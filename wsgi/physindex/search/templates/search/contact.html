Contact
You are welcome to email us at any time with any comments, questions, or concerns that you have about PhysIndex. Feedback from our users helps us grow and we really appreciate it.
Bug Reporting
PhysIndex is currently released as a public beta. This means that feedback about issues with the web site is extremely helpful in making this a finished project. Please be descriptive when reporting a bug, and include information such as the page you were on, what you were trying to do when the issue occured, when it happened, your browser, and your operating system.

Please note that PhysIndex currently only contains information that would be present in an introductory mechanics course. You are welcome to report a specific variable or equation from this topic as missing, but please do not report missing information from more advanced topics like electricity and magnetism, quantum, thermodynamics, et cetera, as we hope to add these later.
